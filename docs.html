How it Works

STMZ AI uses advanced deep learning models to analyze your audio files and separate them into individual "stems". Unlike traditional filters, our AI identifies specific instruments and vocals, allowing for clean isolation with minimal artifacts.

Supported stems include:

  • Acapella: Pure vocal isolation.
  • Instrumental: The full track without vocals.
  • Drums: Percussive elements.
  • Bass: Low-frequency musical elements.
  • Melody: Synths, guitars, and other melodic instruments.

Importing from Rekordbox

To process your Rekordbox library, you first need to export your collection or a specific playlist as an XML file from Rekordbox.

  1. In Rekordbox, go to File > Export Collection in XML format.
  2. Open STMZ AI and click the Rekordbox Tab.
  3. Select your exported XML file. STMZ AI will now display your entire library with all metadata and analysis intact.
  4. Select the tracks you wish to split and click Process.

Exporting to Rekordbox

Once processing is complete, STMZ AI generates high-quality stems and creates a new Rekordbox-compatible XML file.

  1. In STMZ AI settings, ensure your Output Path is set correctly.
  2. After processing, click the Export XML button.
  3. In Rekordbox, go to Preferences > View > Layout and ensure "rekordbox xml" is checked under the Layout section.
  4. In the Rekordbox browser, go to the rekordbox xml section, right-click, and select Refresh.
  5. Your new stems will appear in a dedicated playlist, ready to be dragged into your main collection.
